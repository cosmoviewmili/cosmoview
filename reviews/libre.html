Libre YSL Fragrance

Vienna

This summer I visited a wonderful Austrian city, Vienna. And while strolling through the Stephansplatz, I decided to spoil myself with a little gift, and entered the Douglas store to buy a perfume.

YSL

Usually I am not sure which one to choose, but this time I could not help myself but to go with the YSL’s Libre perfume. YSL Libre smells amazing. It’s sweet but also strong, so it feels comforting but still bold. What I love is that it lasts all day—seriously, one spritz and you’re good. It's definitely versatile, too. You can wear it to school, work, or out at night. It’s the kind of scent that gives off this effortless, confident vibe. If you’re into perfumes that feel fresh but also warm and a little spicy, Libre is definitely worth trying!
