Nudestix Mascara

This product had been waiting for me when I came home after a long vacation and thank you for sending me another complementary Nudestix product. This time, I received Nudestix Nudies Matte Cream Bronzer Stick in the shade Sunkissed Cool. I have tried it a couple of times and I am impressed with the results.
