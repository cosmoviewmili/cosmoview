Very impressed with Consistency

The bronzer has a smooth and creamy consistency that glides effortlessly onto the skin, making it easy to apply and blend.

Longevity

Unlike some other bronzers that can leave a shimmery or sparkly effect, this bronzer stick provides a beautiful, natural-looking matte finish. Once applied, it stays put throughout the day, even in humid weather conditions. It does not fade or become patchy over time, which is amazing!
